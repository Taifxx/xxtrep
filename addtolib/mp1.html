About:

Add-on to add links to the TV shows and movies to Kodi video library from other add-ons as local added videos. Add-on creates links to episodes and movies as .strm files and places them in 'TV shows' and 'Movies' folders.












Added movies and TV Shows scanned by scraper. After that you can see it in Kodi Movies and Kodi TV Shows categories with posters, fanart, watched status etc.

Additional features for TV Shows:

When you add episodes of new or exists TV show automatically added 'episodes source' (a link to the folder from which you add episodes). The add-on can scan this folder (source) and report about new episodes.

For the TV show manually can be added 'seasons source' (a link to folder containing a list of the seasons of the TV show). In this case, the add-on will scan folder for the presence of the new season.
