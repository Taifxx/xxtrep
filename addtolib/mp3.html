Other actions:

'Show all exists TV shows' - Displays a list of exists TV shows added via Add to Lib. When you select the TV show he becomes the current TV show.
'Re-index TV shows' - Rescan TV shows for restore the inner table of links, allowing to define the TV shows from source link or title.
'Restore TV show' - Restore lost episodes of the TV show, in the event of accidental deletion or rename (change the type of URL).
'Open source' - Allows you to go to the source folder.
'Rescan current source' - Replace already added episodes of source on actual episodes of source (useful in case of change of the links within a folder).
'Advanced add' - Add episodes (TV show season) with advanced settings.


    (!)   For stable operation of the add-on, to physically remove or rename the TV shows use the tools of this add-on.
